FAQ

Any Questions? Answered

Running a Money Service Business can be stressful. Our product aims to make it easy, efficient, and reduces risk.

It's a trained AI model designed to make check processing faster while providing a layer of security to detect fraud checks. Additionally, it makes customer and company onboarding easy by automating repeated tasks.
LytCheck leverages multiple databases to gather information about check history, including details about account ACH capabilities and past incidents like Non-sufficient Funds (NSF), Stopped Payment, and Closed or Open accounts. It also provides Fund Assessment to evaluate funds in check issuers' accounts.
Failure to file compliance reports such as Currency Transaction Reports (CTR) or Suspicious Activity Reports (SAR) can result in heavy fines and audits by agencies. We simplify this process by tracking all your transactions and allowing you to file CTR or SAR reports with just one click. This ensures that your paperwork is processed easily and efficiently.
It's a chatroom designed to find information about companies and customers. The chatroom also provides information related to new regulations and various other aspects of Money Service Businesses.
Our chatroom is a community for Money Service Business owners to report bad checks in their area. All reported bad checks are automatically updated in our database. When processing a check from a reported company, you'll receive an alert to prevent financial loss.
