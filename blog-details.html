Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Lytcheck Technology LLC DBA LytCheck operates the websites lytcheck.com and is committed to protecting your privacy. Please review our privacy policy to understand how we handle your information. Our Terms of Use are also available for your reference.

Scope of Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how LytCheck collects and uses your information, including data related to your use of our products and services. This policy does not cover practices of companies we do not own or control, nor does it apply to people we do not employ or manage. Some of our partners may have their own privacy policies.

Personal Information

LytCheck collects personal information when you visit our websites, join our community, sign up for newsletters, request information, participate in contests, complete surveys, or purchase our products and services. Personal information includes data that can identify you, such as your name, address, email address, and phone number, which are not publicly available. We use this information to create your account, send requested information, and fulfill orders.

When you join our community, you need to provide an email address and can opt into our mailing lists. If you purchase our products or services, we may add you to our mailing lists and occasionally contact you via email. For contests or surveys, we may request your name, address, and email to manage the event and notify winners.

Use of Personal Information

LytCheck does not sell or rent your personal information. If referred to our site by a partner, we may share basic registration information with that partner. We may also release personal information when required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of LytCheck, our users, or others, including for fraud protection and credit risk management. Note that our mailing lists may be archived by third-party sites over which we have no control.

Community Information

Information shared by members of the LytCheck community, including sample data, event types, tags, comments, and other contributions to our Knowledge Base, may be reused by other community members under the terms of the LytCheck Creative Commons License.

Aggregate Information

LytCheck collects aggregated tracking data from website visitors, such as page views, traffic flows, search terms, and click-throughs, to improve our websites. This anonymous traffic and demographic information may be shared in aggregate form with advertisers and business partners without identifying individual users.

Cookies

LytCheck uses cookies to enhance your experience on our websites by storing and sometimes tracking information about you. Cookies are small data files sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your hard drive. For instance, a cookie is sent when you sign up or log in to our websites. With your consent, cookies can save information from forms to create a user profile, eliminating the need to re-enter data on subsequent visits.

You can configure your browser to notify you whenever a cookie is received, allowing you to accept or reject them. However, rejecting cookies may limit the functionality of our websites.

IP Addresses

Our websites automatically capture IP addresses, which we use to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movement, ensure security, and gather broad demographic information.

Security

LytCheck employs security measures to protect your information from loss, misuse, and alteration. All personal and aggregate information is stored on a server located in a secured facility, accessible only to authorized personnel.

Notification of Changes

If we update our Privacy Policy, we will post the changes here. If we intend to use your personally identifiable information differently from when it was collected, we will notify you via email, allowing you to opt-out of any new use.

Choice/Opt-Out

You can opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at any time by following the instructions provided in our communications or contacting us directly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or comments about our privacy policy, practices, or your interactions with LytCheck, please contact us using contact form.
